Project Arm of Malenia

I am Malnia, blade of Miqella

If you have played Elden Ring and continued to the Haligtree that phrase is engraved upon your soul. Death after death only to finally get some momentum going before being waterfowled into the dirt. We've all been there. But there are a few who persevered and conquered the fight as a true Tarnished would.

I am happy to count myself among the number who defeated Malenia without the use of summons, elemental effects, or any other "cheese tactics". After beating Malenia I decided I wanted a memento of the fight and what better memento than the arm of Malenia herself?

Turns out the official prop is expensive so I bought a stl file online and printed it. The top left image is the first piece I printed, the hand. I mainly used the Prusa XL 5 toolhead printer to get a big enough footprint for some of the parts. I then did some post processing and spraypainted each part golden.

Currently I am in the process of adding a dark wash to the each of the pieces to give the arm a tarnished (heh) look.

This project is in stage 5
