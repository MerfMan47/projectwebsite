Project Samsung TV Blue

Long story short my family had a Samsung TV that randomly gained a blue tint one day. It had nothing to do with the picture settings as even the startup logo was tinted blue. After some investigating I determined that the cause must be the TVs backlight. Turns out Samsung TVs are known to have an issue where the white LEDS on the backlight burn out leaving only the blue giving images a characteristic blue tint. So I ordered some backlight strips off of eBay and disassembled the TV.

The images show the backlights behind the TV screen and the replacement parts I ordered.

To reach the backlight I was required to entirely dissemble the TV pulling apart the shell, taking out the power and logic boards, carefully removing the screen then the diffusing screens before I could access the backlight. A quick backlight swap and reassembly later and I powered on the TV to find that the blue tint had vanished.

This was a fun repair project as not many require a complete disassembly of the device. I feel confident that I could easily do it again if necessary.

This project is in stage 6
